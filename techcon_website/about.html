History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for developers and engineers passionate about innovation in hardware and software technologies. Over the years, it has grown into one of the world’s most influential technology conferences, attracting global leaders, creators, and pioneers in the tech industry.

Mission

Our mission is to bring together visionaries and innovators to share ideas, inspire future advancements, and foster collaboration across different disciplines of technology. TechCon is committed to pushing boundaries, empowering creators, and shaping the future of global tech culture.
